This script is for running with Hyper-V enabled:

qemu-system-x86_64 -machine pc -accel whpx -smp 12 -m 4G \
    -drive file=/ucrt64/share/qemu/edk2-x86_64-code.fd,if=pflash,format=raw \
    -drive file='\\.\PhysicalDrive1',format=raw \
    -device virtio-gpu-pci -display gtk

Without Hyper-V, you can change whpx to tcg.

wait for 15 seconds, then type
fs0:\EFI\BOOT\BOOTX64.efi
